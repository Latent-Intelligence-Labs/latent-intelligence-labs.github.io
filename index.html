LatentLM

LatentLM is our flagship model – a radical reimagining of the language model. It doesn’t just continue text. It **understands**, **transforms**, and **responds** using high-dimensional vector representations. With separate encoder, latent manipulator, and decoder modules, LatentLM is designed to think before it speaks.
